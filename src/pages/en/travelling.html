Location

Riverwash 8 will be held between 2nd and 4th of September (from Friday to Sunday) in a theatre Teatr Bez Sceny, located at 3 Maja 11, Katowice, Poland.

There are multiple accommodation facilities in Katowice and almost all of them are ready to serve foreign tourists so you do not have to worry if they speak English. If you have any questions related to our party, feel free to contact the organizers either through our Facebook fanpage or email (contact@riverwash.org).

Travelling

You can get the party-place:

  • by car — if you come from the west or east, choose the A4 motorway, from the south — A1, from the north — DK1.
  • by plane — we recommend to book your flight either to Katowice-Pyrzowice airport (nearby Katowice) or Cracow (bus trip from Cracow to Katowice takes only 1h15m and buses travel between these cities every 15 minutes). If you need any assistance with travelling, contact us!
  • by train — search for local train connections here.

Accommodation

You have the following options:

  • at the party place — included in the entrance ticket. The party place will be noisy, so earplugs are advised.
  • near the party place — literally hundreds of hotels, hostels and apartments in the city. We do not advertise any of them but if you have problems finding accommodation, we can help.

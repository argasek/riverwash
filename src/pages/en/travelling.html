Location

Riverwash 10 will be held between 31st August and 2nd of September (from Friday to Sunday) in a restaurant Drzwi Zwane Koniem, Warszawska 37 in Katowice, Poland.

There are multiple accommodation facilities in Katowice and almost all of them are ready to serve foreign tourists so you do not have to worry if they speak English. If you have any questions related to our party, feel free to contact the organizers either through our Facebook fanpage or email (contact@riverwash.org).

Travelling

You can get the party-place:

  • by car — if you come from the west or east, choose the A4 motorway, from the south — A1, from the north — DK1. Attention! Car access to the party place is not possible (a pedestrian area). We suggest you to leave your car at your place of accommodation or at a public car park.
  • by plane — we recommend to book your flight either to Katowice-Pyrzowice airport (nearby Katowice) or Cracow (bus trip from Cracow to Katowice takes only 1h15m and buses travel between these cities every 15 minutes). If you need any assistance with travelling, contact us!
  • by train — search for local train connections here.

Free, unattended car parks:

  • at the University of Silesa (near Rawa river), entry from streets: Dudy-Gracza, Warszawska and Pawła – Wodna – Górnicza area
  • Plac Wolności (square), entry from Sądowa and Marii Goeppert-Mayer, before the Mikołowska – Słowackiego – Matejki crossroads
  • Plac Sejmu Śląskiego (square)

Accommodation

You have the following options:

  • at the party place — if you want to sleep at the party place, we have nothing against. Just remember we can't provide you with appropriate conditions for a human being (bed, shower, etc.) and it can be noisy. You have been warned!
  • near the party place — literally hundreds of hotels, hostels and apartments in the city. We do not advertise any of them but if you have problems finding accommodation, we can help.

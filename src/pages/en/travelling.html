Location

Riverwash 7 will be held between 4th and 6th of September (from Friday to Sunday) in ‘Kawiarnia Naukowa’ Pub, Cracow (Wesoła district), Poland.

Cracow is the second biggest city in Poland, former capital and a huge culture centre, located in the south of Poland. Cracow is the most popular destination in Poland for foreign tourists as it features numerous world-famous landmarks and sightseeing spots, including the Wawel Castle, the salt mine in nearby Wieliczka and many more. Kazimierz district is the heart of nightlife of the city with hundreds of cafes, restaurants and pubs.

There are multiple accommodation facilities in Cracow and almost all of them are ready to serve foreign tourists so you do not have to worry if they speak English. If you have any questions related to our party, feel free to contact the organizers either through our Facebook fanpage or email (contact@riverwash.org).

Travelling

Party place address: ‘Kawiarnia Naukowa’ Pub, Stanisława Żółkiewskiego 19 Street, Cracow (Wesoła district), Poland — see on Google Maps.

You can get the party-place:

  • by car — if you come from the west or east, choose the A4 motorway. Motorways in Poland aren’t free. If you travel from the west, you’ll have to pay for Wroclaw-Katowice and Katowice-Cracow sections (around €4 each, one way). Payments are received at the toll gates, no vignette system. The Cracow ring road section and A4 eastwards from there is free of charge. There is a watched car park nearby and couple of others on Kazimierz district. Prices vary, usually €10-15 a night.
  • by plane — there’s an airport directly in Cracow (not a remote airfield in the middle of nowhere) and the transfer from there to the centre by bus (lines: 208, 292, 902), taxi and other means (private bus service, etc.). We are going to publish a detailed description of this route on our Facebook page so watch that space. You can also fly to Katowice-Pyrzowice (approx. 100 km, ~2h by bus to Cracow) or Warsaw-Okęcie (approx. 300 km, ~3h by train to Cracow). If you need any assistance with travelling, contact us!
  • by train — search for local train connections here.

Accommodation

You have the following options:

  • at the party place — included in the entrance ticket. There are several sofas and a separate room where you can rest but it will be noisy, so earplugs are advised.
  • near the party place — literally hundreds of hotels, hostels and apartments in the city. We do not advertise any of them but if you have problems finding accommodation, we can help.

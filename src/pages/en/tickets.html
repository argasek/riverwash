Entrance fee

We offer 2 types of tickets:

  • participant ticket — 100 PLN (approx. €25) — grants entry for the whole party (Friday-Sunday) with the right to vote and take part in the competitions. The price also includes free breakfast on Saturday and Sunday morning.
  • supporter ticker — >100 PLN — if you like our party, you can pay more than the regular fee and support our hardware team and future Riverwash events. We are preparing a special thank-you gift for all supporters! The aforementioned breakfast is of course included.
  • special discount - 50% discount for foreigners who travel at least 700 km to get to the party place.

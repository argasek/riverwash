Entrance fee

We offer 2 various types of tickets:

  • participant ticket — 100 PLN (approx. €25) — grants entry for the whole party (Friday-Sunday) with the right to vote and take part in the competitions.
  • supporter ticker — >100 PLN — if you like our party, you can pay more than the regular fee and support our hardware team and future Riverwash events. We are preparing a special thank-you gift for all supporters!

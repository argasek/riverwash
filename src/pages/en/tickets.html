Entrance fee

We offer 2 various types of tickets and a special 50% discount:

  • participant ticket — 100 PLN (approx. €25) — grants entry for the whole party (Friday-Sunday) with the right to vote and take part in the competitions.
  • supporter ticker — >100 PLN — if you like our party, you can pay more than the regular fee and support our hardware team and future Riverwash events. We are preparing a special thank-you gift for all supporters!
  • contributor discount 50% — if you submit a coded entry. If it’s a demo, the discount is for up to 3 people who made it (they have to be in the credits). In case of intros, the discount is valid for 1 person (the coder). Executables have to be submitted before the deadline and qualify for showing. The discounts will be reimbursed during the prizegiving.

Slow Compo

It’s still a long time till this year’s Riverwash but we’re already launching the first compo! Have a look at the rules and start preparing your entry:

  • The aim of the compo is to create an entry using materials from the previous edition of Riverwash.
  • In order to get qualified, you need to use at least one audio file from a pack prepared by the organizers.
  • Your entry can be a demo, animation or even a live stage performance. We count on your creativity!
  • The entries will be presented in a separate compo during the Riverwash 2016 competitions.
  • The first prize is a free entrance to the party (refunded).
  • You can find the pack with samples here.

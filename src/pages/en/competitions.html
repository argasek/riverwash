Competitions

After years of joint multiplatform competitions due to the small number of entries, we have noticed an improvement trend and we are really happy with that! This year we are introducing separate coded compos for every viable platform. If any of the platforms has less than 3 entries in any compo, we are going to join demos and intros together and, if this is not enough, use the former oldschool / newschool formula. We will also be flexible with other compos if they are very popular (separating different kinds of tracker formats if needed, etc.), all to make the competitions more exciting. As this is a multiplatform party, any demoscene hardware is welcome.

Categories

  • demo compo (separate for each platform)
  • intro compo (separate for each platform)
  • graphics (freestyle)
  • graphics (pixel)
  • music (streaming)
  • music (chip)
  • wild/animation
  • crazy demo

and non-competitive “your old demo compo” (details in Rules).

Rules

“Lucky dragon” is our theme this year (because Riverwash 7, which is a lucky number and dragon is the symbol of Cracow). We encourage you to include the theme in your prod. The best and most clever uses will be rewarded with a sur-prize.

General rules

  • Remote entries are allowed, see How to submit your entry
  • Only new entries, not submitted anywhere else
  • No copy / no timbaland / no moroder
  • We reserve the right to reject/remove any entries which do not follow the rules or are not demoscene related
  • If your prod violates any Polish or international law, is considered offensive or inappropriate, we can disqualify it or exclude it from post-party submission to demoscene archives

Freestyle GFX

  • any resolution (our beamer is 1920x1080 max)
  • 3D renders, shopped pictures and all which does not qualify for the Pixel GFX category
  • popular file format (IrfanView will be used)
  • please submit work stages

Pixel GFX

  • resolution: 320 x 256 max, 256 colours max
  • please submit work stages
  • in case of Atari/C-64/ZX Spectrum entries please submit an executable file (*.xex, *.prg, *d64 or*.tap), can also be a 5.25” floppy or *.d64 files (C-64).

Freestyle MSX

  • allowed formats: MP3/Ogg Vorbis
  • max playtime – 3 minutes (entries can be longer but only the initial 3 minutes will be played at the party)

Chip MSX

  • synthesis-based formats: AHX/THX/HVL (Amiga), SID (C-64, PRG or PSID), SNG (GoatTracker), VTX, PT3 and other played by Vortex Tracker II (ZX Spectrum/AY), RMT (Pokey), FTM/NSF (FamiTracker/NES), MGB/GBS (GameBoy), TFE (TFM Music Maker), A2M and others played by Adlib Tracker II (YMF262).
  • max playtime – 3 minutes (entries can be longer but only the initial 3 minutes will be played at the party).
  • in case of SID format, please give SID version info in the file and provide a C-64 executable or playSID file.
  • if your format is not listed, please contact us before the party.

Intro (256B / 1 KiB / 4 KiB / 64 KiB)

  • maximum file size is 64 KiB (65536 bytes)
  • separate categories for different platforms / file sizes if a category is popular (at least 3 entries)
  • only executables
  • in case of C-64 please submit a .d64 or .prg file and provide SID version info
  • if your platform is not listed, please contact us before the party or bring your own hardware
  • all platforms except for PC will have prods recorded to a loseless video file and played from it during the compo for technical reasons. We record videos only from real hardware, no emulators allowed.

Demo

  • executables with file sizes over 64 KiB
  • separate categories for different platforms / file sizes if a category is popular (at least 3 entries)
  • all exotic libraries should be provided in the same archive
  • in case of C-64 please submit a .d64 or .prg file and provide SID version info
  • if your platform is not listed, please contact us before the party or bring your own hardware
  • all platforms except for PC will have prods recorded to a loseless video file and played from it during the compo for technical reasons. We record videos only from real hardware, no emulators allowed.

Wild / animation

  • all entries which cannot be qualified for other competitions: films, anims, exotic platforms, music discs, slideshows, etc. Crazy compo is a separate category and it is up to the authors to decide if their entry is serious or not.
  • all entries should be provided in one of popular video formats (AVI/MP4) or an executable.
  • if you use strange codecs, bring them with you.

Your old demo compo (as seen at Xperience 2013 and Riverwash 2014)

Show your old demo which did not win at another party. Did people vote for their friends? Were voters too drunk to appreciate your work? You have another chance to show your precious!

  • any author of the demo has to be present at the party
  • the prod has to be delivered in any offline format before the compo (please do not ask us to download them for you from scene.org, YouTube, etc.)
  • you have to tell your story before the showing! Tell us why you chose this demo, why it is worth watching and why it did not win. If you don’t speak up — we won’t show it!
  • no winners in this compo, no voting, just nostalgia
  • one entry per one group

How to submit your entry

  • entries at the party should be physically submitted to the compo team (pendrive, USB HDD, CD, floppy) before the deadline
  • remote entries are allowed but you will not receive the statue as we are not going to send it via post. In case of a remote submission, please email us (fei@amiscena.org) an attachment or a link to a cloud service of your choice. The deadline for remote entries is 03.09.2015.
  • only compressed archives (zip/7zip) with the following naming pattern: componame_nick/group_title.zip (newschoolgfx_george_landscape.zip)
  • please include an info file with details in the archive

The categories for unusual or tongue-in-cheek productions.

Slow Compo

  • The aim of the compo is to create an entry using materials from the previous edition of Riverwash
  • In order to get qualified, you need to use at least one audio file from a pack prepared by the organizers
  • Your entry can be a demo, animation or even a live stage performance. We count on your creativity!
  • Details to be announced soon!

Crazy Demo

  • Production of a "crazy demo" nature, humorous, ironic, horse-like, etc. :).
  • All entries should be provided in one of popular video formats (AVI/MP4) or an executable.
  • If you use strange codecs, bring them with you.

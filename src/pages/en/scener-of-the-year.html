Polish Demoscener Of The Year

During Riverwash 2015 we held a "Polish Demoscene All-Star" ceremony, being an award for a person, who — in our opinion — deserves a great deal of respect for his/her overall demoscene activity and input. A person chosen by the demoparty organizers was Kierownik^Elude for his overall achievements on the Amiga demoscene. This year we changed the name a little bit, but most importantly — as we had promised — we left the choice to the public.

Hereby we open the public submission of candidates for the Polish Demoscener Of The Year awards! In the form below please fill in the nickname of the Polish, active demoscener (for at least the last two years) on any platform, who — in your opinion — deserves this title the most (along with a short description why do you think so). You may nominate your candidate till the end of July. In the August we will present the candidates you have chosen on the website.

Next, demosceners with the biggest number of submissions will get to the 2nd stage of voting — which will be held on this year's Riverwash — each demoparty attendant will have a chance to cast a vote (electronic or a paper one). A demoscener with the biggest number of votes will get the Polish Demoscener Of The Year title and receive a surprise prize.

Information

Demoscene — informal movement of digital content creators who produce graphics, music and software (demos/intros) — non-interactive presentations of (mostly 3D) graphics calculated in real time. Demoscene productions are distributed free of charge and without license limitations. More about the demoscene:

  • http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Demoscene
  • http://www.pouet.net

Demoparty — a meeting of demoscene related people and their friends during which competitions are run in several categories. Parties have been organized in Poland for 25 years.

Riverwash — a multiplatform demoparty organized by the Polish demoscene, currently the biggest event of that kind in Poland, for all platforms. Riverwash is held for the seventh time. Previous parties:

  • 24-26 August 2007, Szymocice n/Racibórz
  • 29-31 August 2008, Rudy Raciborskie
  • 4-6 September 2009, Warsaw
  • 3-5 September 2010, Warsaw
  • 2-4 September 2011, Łódź
  • 19-21 September 2014, Racibórz

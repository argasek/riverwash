Wild / Animation

The most unhampered competition category. Unleash your creativity, release the Kraken!

  • All entries which cannot be qualified for other competitions: films, anims, exotic platforms, music discs, slideshows, etc. Crazy demo is a separate category and it is up to the authors to decide if their entry is serious or not.
